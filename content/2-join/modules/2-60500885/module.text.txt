Title: Membership Benefits

----

Text: 

###**The benefits of membership:**
- Join the oldest professional anthropological organization in the United States.
- Be a member of a thriving group of anthropologists who organize an annual meeting and publish the journal American Ethnologist.
- Carry on a variety of conversations and activities that promote broad-ranging scholarship on human culture.
- Have access to AES monographs at reduced prices.
- Hear directly about AES events, news, and competitions.
- Create and take stock of the pulse of anthropology.
