    #AES Student Diversity Travel Grant
    
    These grants are intended to increase participation in (link: http://americanethnologist.org/meetings/spring-conference/aes-2019 text: AES's annual spring conference in St. Louis) (March 14-16, 2019) by graduate students from historically under-represented groups. Students are required to present a paper (application portal closes January 28, 2019).
    
    Graduate students from historically underrepresented groups and those with disabilities are encouraged to apply for these travel grants to help defray costs associated with attending the AES conference.
    
    **Grants of up to $500 each will be awarded (in the form of reimbursement for travel, lodging, and/or meals, upon submission of receipts).**
    
    **Travel Grant Applications are due by January 28, 2019.**
    
    Send to AESGrantsCompetition@gmail.com. Please include your last name followed by "AES diversity" in the title of the attachment.
    
    Please include the following with your application:
    - A completed cover sheet: (link: http://americanethnologist.org/content/8-news/20181211-call-for-proposals-aes-student-diversity-travel-grant/aes-diversity-application-final.doc text: [download here])
    - A brief CV (maximum 2 pages)
    - Letter of reference from a scholar or advisor who knows your work (maximum 2 pages). Please ask that the recommender submit the letter directly to AESGrantsCompetition@gmail.com by the deadline, January 28, 2019.
    
    For questions, contact AESGrantsCompetition@gmail.com.
