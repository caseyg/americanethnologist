# #Ferguson Supplement: Activist Resources

Online platforms have recently become both tools for connecting "(link: http://thisisthemovement.launchrock.com/ text: words to actions)" and sites of political participation and real-time engagement. During #Ferguson a new generation of young activists forged new forms of community and modes of digital engagement.

- (link: http://www.wetheprotesters.org/ text: "We the Protesters" Organizing hub for Ferguson Protesters with lists of demands and policy statements)
- (link: https://blacklivesmatteraaa.wordpress.com/ text: "Black Lives Matters AAA" organizing group)
- (link: http://blacklivesmatter.com/ text: Black Lives Matter)
- (link: http://www.handsupunited.org/ text: Hands Up United)
- (link: http://www.blackoutforhumanrights.com/ text: Black Out for Human Rights)
- (link: http://colorofchange.org/ text: Color of Change)
- (link: http://thisisthemovement.launchrock.com/ text: This is the Movement - Activist Newsletter featuring articles, quotes, videos, resources, and ways to get involved.)
- (link: http://fergusonresponse.tumblr.com/ text: Ferguson Response - Nationwide listing of protests against police brutality and racial injustice)
