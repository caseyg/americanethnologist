Title: Sharon Stephens Prize

----

Text: Ethnographies and critical works in contemporary theory–single-authored or multi-authored but not edited collections–are eligible. The Sharon Stephens Prize is awarded at the AES business meeting at the AAA annual meeting.

----

Intro: The Sharon Stephens Prize is awarded bi-annually for a junior scholar’s first book. The prize ($1000) goes to a work that speaks to contemporary social issues with relevance beyond the discipline and beyond the academy.
