Congratulations to Ellen Sharp (UC Los Angeles), winner of the 2013 Elsie Clews Parsons Prize (for best graduate student paper) for her paper “The Gun that Backfires: Violence and the Paradox of Neoliberal Multiculturalism.” Honorable mention went to Nicolas Lainez (EHESS) for “Commodified Sexuality and Mother-Daughter Power Dynamics in the Mekong Delta.” Read here about the two winning papers and about next year's competition: (link: http://www.anthropology-news.org/index.php/2013/05/23/aess-2013-elsie-clews-parsons-prize/ text: http://www.anthropology-news.org/index.php/2013/05/23/aess-2013-elsie-clews-parsons-prize/)
