)Marc Sommersis an internationally recognized youth expert with research experience in over twenty war-affected countries. *The Outcast Majority: War, Development, and Youth in Africa *was published in 2015 (University of Georgia Press), and was selected for Honorable Mention by the prize committee: Marc Edelman (co-chair, CUNY), Shalini Shankar (co-chair, Northwestern U), Tine Gammeltoft (U Copenhagen), and Elizabeth Ferry (Brandeis U).
