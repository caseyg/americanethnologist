- **2016 Exemplary Cross-Field Scholarship Award by the AAA's General Anthropology Division** to **Radhika Govindrajan** for her 2015 article “(link: http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/amet.12144/full text: 'The Goat that Died for Family': Animal Sacrifice and Interspecies Kinship in India's Central Himalayas),” *American Ethnologist* 42 (3): 504-519. <figure>

- **2013 Boyer Prize awarded by the Society for Psychological Anthropology** to **John Borneman** for his 2011 article “(link: http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1548-1425.2011.01303.x/abstract text: Daydreaming, Intimacy, and the Intersubjective Third in Fieldwork Encounters in Syria),” *American Ethnologist* 38 (2):234-248.
- **2012 Anthropology and the Environment Section award** to **Shaylih Muehlmann** for her May 2012 article, "(link: http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1548-1425.2012.01368.x/abstract text: Rhizomes and Other Uncountables: The Malaise of Enumeration in Mexico's Colorado River Delta), " *American Ethnologist* 39 (2):339-353. (link: http://www.aaanet.org/sections/ae/index.php/colin-west-interviews-shaylih-muehlman/ text: Here is an interview about Muehlmann's article).
- **2012 General Anthropology Division Prize for Exemplary Cross-Field Scholarship** awarded to **Rheana Parreñas** for her November 2012 article, "(link: http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1548-1425.2012.01387.x/abstract text: Producing Affect: Transnational Volunteerism in a Malaysian Orangutan Rehabilitation Center), " *American Ethnologist* 39 (4):673–687.
- **2012 Runner-up for General Anthropology Division Prize for Exemplary Cross-Field Scholarship** was **Duana Fullwiley**, for her November 2010 article, "(link: http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1548-1425.2010.01276.x/abstract text: Revaluating Genetic Causation: Biology, Economy,and Kinship in Dakar, Senegal), " *American Ethnologist* 37 (4):638–661.
- **2011 Boyer Prize awarded by the Society for Psychological Anthropology to Kevin P. Groark** for his 2009 article, “(link: http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1548-1425.2009.01205.x/abstract text: Discourses of the Soul: The Negotiation of Personal Agency in Tzotzil Maya Dream Narrative),” *American Ethnologist* 36 (4):705–721.
