 “Ethnography at Its Edges,” a new feature in *American Ethnologist*, presents review essays that explore the relationship between the use of ethnographic techniques in disciplines other than anthropology and contemporary trends within the discipline. The first two essays look at how anthropology overlaps with and differs from journalism and fiction.
